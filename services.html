Our Services

We offer end-to-end property management solutions designed specifically for the needs of Non-Resident Indians.

Rental Management

Finding the right tenant is just the beginning. We handle the entire lifecycle of tenancy so you don't have to deal with late night calls or payment follow-ups.

  • Tenant background verification & police clearance
  • Rental agreement drafting & registration
  • Monthly rent collection & deposit
  • Tenant relationship management
  • Move-in & move-out inspections
Image: Happy Tenants

Maintenance & Repairs

Keep your property in showroom condition. We coordinate all repairs with trusted local vendors and supervise the work.

  • Regular cleaning & deep cleaning services
  • Plumbing, electrical, and carpentry repairs
  • Painting and waterproofing
  • Pest control services
  • Appliance servicing (AC, RO, Geyser)
Image: Maintenance Work

Legal & Documentation

Navigate Indian property laws with ease. Our legal experts ensure your property is compliant and secure.

  • Property tax payments
  • Society maintenance bill management
  • Khata transfer & mutation assistance
  • Power of Attorney (PoA) coordination
  • Legal dispute advisory
Image: Legal Documents

Resale & Renovation

Whether you want to upgrade your home or sell it for the best price, we act as your ground team.

  • Full-scale home renovation supervision
  • Interior design coordination
  • Property valuation services
  • Buyer search and negotiation for resale
  • Sale deed registration support
